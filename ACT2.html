Homemade Pork Siomai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg ground pork
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup minced onions
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Siomai or wonton wrappers

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground pork, carrots, onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, egg, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place a spoonful of the mixture in the center of each wrapper.
  3. Fold and shape the wrapper to form a cup, leaving the top open.
  4. Arrange siomai pieces in a steamer, making sure they don’t touch each other.
  5. Steam for 20–25 minutes or until fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with soy sauce, calamansi, and chili garlic oil.

Enjoy your delicious homemade siomai!
